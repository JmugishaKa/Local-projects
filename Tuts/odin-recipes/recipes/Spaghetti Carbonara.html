Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

Description:

A classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese (Pecorino Romano or Parmesan), pancetta or bacon, and black pepper. It's creamy, comforting, and surprisingly quick to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or bacon
  • 2 whole eggs + 2 yolks
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan
  • Salt and freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)
  1. Step 1: Cook the Pasta

    2. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

  2. Step 2: Fry the Pancetta

    3. In a pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crisp. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. Step 3: Mix the Egg Sauce

    4. In a bowl, whisk eggs, yolks, cheese, and a good amount of black pepper.

  4. Step 4: Combine Pasta and Sauce

    5. Quickly toss hot pasta with pancetta and fat. Gradually mix in egg-cheese mixture while stirring vigorously. Add reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.

  5. Step 5: Serve

    6. Plate immediately with extra cheese and pepper. Serve hot.
