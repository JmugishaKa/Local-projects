Vegetarian Stir-Fry

Home

Description:

A colorful and healthy mix of sautéed vegetables tossed in a savory sauce. This dish is quick, versatile, and perfect for weeknight meals. You can serve it with rice or noodles.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), sliced
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • 1/2 cup water or vegetable broth
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving
  1. Step 1: Prepare the Sauce

    2. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, cornstarch (optional), and water or broth. Stir well and set aside.

  2. Step 2: Stir-Fry the Vegetables

    3. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 30 seconds. Add carrots and broccoli and cook for 2–3 minutes.

  3. Step 3: Add Remaining Veggies

    4. Toss in bell pepper and snap peas. Stir-fry all together until vegetables are just tender (about 3–4 more minutes).

  4. Step 4: Add Sauce

    5. Pour in the prepared sauce. Stir constantly until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables (1–2 minutes).

  5. Step 5: Serve

    6. Remove from heat and serve immediately over cooked rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.
